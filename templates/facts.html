Interesting Facts

1.Naismith was a Canadian-American sports coach asked by Springfield College Massachusetts to come up with a winter sport to keep athletes fit.

2. It has recently been claimed that Naismith's original game involved rolling the ball along the ground before throwing it into a basket.

3. The idea of passing the ball by throwing it in the air has been attributed to Lambert G Will, a YMCA director in Herkimer, New York.

4. The original basketball basket was closed at the bottom; a ladder was needed to recover the ball.

5. Repeated bouncing of the ball (dribbling) was illegal in Naismith's original rules.

6. The first game under his rules was in 1892.

7. Basketball is the national sort of Lithuania and Georgia. It is the summer national sport in Latvia, where ice hockey is the winter national sport.

8. Basketball has been in every summer Olympics since 1936. The USA has won 14 times, the USSR twice and Argentina and Yugoslavia once each.

9. Hospital emergency rooms in the US in 2011 treated 249,650 cases aged 12-17 for injuries sustained by playing basketball.
